About Whamr

The sound of a perfect meme landing

Wham is not a gentle ping. It is the moment a perfectly timed meme lands in a group chat and everything stops. Someone snorts. Someone drops their phone. The read receipt turns blue before the laughter subsides.

We built Whamr because finding the right meme was taking too long. Too many tabs, too many dead image hosts, too many screenshots buried in camera rolls. The wham was there, but the send was slow.

Now the library covers 11 categories: Reactions, Dance, Laughing, Naija, Sports, Pop Culture, Awkward, Sad, Random, Birthday, and Love. Nine platforms. One tap to send. No account needed to start.

34,100 people are already sending memes. The library grows every week. The wham keeps landing.
