Common questions with straightforward answers. No runaround.
Sharing
WhatsApp, Telegram, X (Twitter), Facebook, Reddit, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Email, and direct download. Tap "Send →" on any meme to see all options.
No. The link works in any browser. Recipients see the meme on a public share page — no account needed.
Uploading
MP4 (video clips), WebP (stickers), GIF (animated), PNG and JPG (images). Max file size: 50 MB.
By default, yes — your meme joins the public library. You can set it to "Private" during upload if you only want to access it yourself.
When you upload, Whamr reads your filename and any title you give it, then automatically suggests tags and a category. You can accept, edit, or add your own.
Creator tools
Upgrade to Creator plan ($7/month). Followers can then tip you from your profile. Earnings are withdrawable monthly, minimum $5.
Yes — on the sticker export page, select up to 30 stickers, choose "WhatsApp format", and tap "Download." Follow the in-app prompts to add the pack to WhatsApp.
Community
A Dojo is a themed community group. Each Dojo has a feed, meme battles, and rules. Join up to 3 Dojos on the free plan, unlimited on Creator.
A battle is a head-to-head vote between two memes. Members of the Dojo vote for their favourite. The winner gets pinned at the top of the Dojo feed for 24h.
