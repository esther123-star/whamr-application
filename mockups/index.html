Design notes & conventions

Images: Real media served from Cloudflare R2 (whamr-media bucket) — MP4 video memes + WebP stickers

Fonts: Space Grotesk (headings, logo) · Inter (body, labels, inputs) · JetBrains Mono (numbers, stats)

Nav height: 64px sticky · white bg · 1px bottom border · pill search bar

Meme card: White · 12px radius · 1px border · shadow-sm · aspect-square image · Send → CTA

Brand colors: #FF4500 deep violet (primary) · #D97706 amber gold (accent/wham)

Content: 245 video memes · 8,455 WebP stickers · 11 categories · 9 share platforms · AI natural language search
