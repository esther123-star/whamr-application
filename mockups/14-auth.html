FAQ
Common questions
Is Whamr really free to use?
Yes. The free tier gives you unlimited meme browsing, sharing to any platform and up to 50 uploads per month. No credit card required at signup.
How do I join a Dojo?
After creating your account, visit the Dojos page and tap Join on any public Dojo. Private Dojos require a request which a moderator must approve. Invite-only Dojos need a direct link from an existing member.
Can I share memes directly to WhatsApp?
Yes. Tap the Send button on any meme and choose WhatsApp, Telegram, X, Facebook or copy the link. The share sheet uses native OS sharing so it works across all apps on your device.
What is a Creator account?
The Creator tier ($9.99/month) unlocks full analytics, revenue sharing on viral content, unlimited Dojo memberships and a verified badge on your profile. Creators also get priority placement in the Explore feed.
How does Whamr handle my data?
Whamr is SOC2 Type II certified. We do not sell your data to third parties. You can export or delete your account data at any time from Settings. We comply with GDPR and Nigerian NDPR data regulations.
What file formats can I upload?
We support JPEG, PNG, GIF and WebP. Maximum file size is 20MB per upload. GIFs are automatically optimised for fast loading. Video uploads are available on Creator and PRO plans (MP4 up to 60 seconds).
