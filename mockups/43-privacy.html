Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 May 2026

We keep this simple. Here's exactly what data we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

Minimal data No ads No data selling You control your data
01

Who We Are

Whamr Ltd ("Whamr", "we", "us", "our") operates the Whamr platform at whamr.app. We are the data controller for personal data processed through the Service.

For data protection enquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer: dpo@whamr.app

02

Data We Collect

2.1 Account data

When you register: email address, username, display name, optional profile photo. We do not require a real name.

2.2 Content data

Memes, stickers, and videos you upload; captions and tags you add; comments and discussion posts you write.

2.3 Usage data

Pages visited, memes viewed, searches performed, share actions (platform chosen but not recipient details), timestamps.

2.4 Device data

Browser type, operating system, IP address (for security purposes). We do not collect precise location.

2.5 Payment data

Creator plan subscribers: payment is processed by Stripe. Whamr does not store full card details. We receive a payment confirmation and your billing country.

2.6 Community data

Discussion posts, Origin and Variants contributions stored in your browser's localStorage. If you are signed in, community contributions may be synced to our servers.

2.7 What we do NOT collect

  • Precise location
  • Contact book or social graph
  • Biometric data
  • Third-party messaging content (when you share to WhatsApp, we send a link — we don't see what happens after)

04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Cloudflare infrastructure (R2, Workers, D1) with servers in the EU and USA. We use TLS encryption for data in transit and AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest.

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. We never store plaintext passwords.

We conduct regular security reviews. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • AccessRequest a copy of your personal data
  • RectificationCorrect inaccurate data
  • ErasureRequest deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • PortabilityReceive your data in a machine-readable format
  • ObjectionObject to processing based on legitimate interest
  • RestrictionRequest we limit how we use your data
  • Withdraw consentAt any time for consent-based processing

To exercise any right, email privacy@whamr.app or use Settings → Account → Data & Privacy.

We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk).

08

Children's Privacy

Whamr is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly.

If you believe a child has registered, please contact: privacy@whamr.app.
