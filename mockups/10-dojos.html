📋 About this Dojo
Naija Meme HQ is the home of Nigerian internet culture on Whamr. We share the funniest Nollywood memes, Pidgin jokes, Afrobeats reactions and everything that makes Nigerians laugh online. From oga at the top to e don do moments, this is your community. Founded in January 2023 by @memequeen, the Dojo has grown to become one of the most active communities on the platform with over 12,000 posts and new memes uploaded daily.
Dojo Rules
  • 1Keep it Naija. Content should relate to Nigerian culture, humour or entertainment.
  • 2No hate speech or tribalism. We celebrate all Nigerian tribes equally.
  • 3Credit original creators when reposting their content.
  • 4No spam or promotional links without admin approval.
  • 5Keep discussions respectful. Banter is fine, but personal attacks are not.
