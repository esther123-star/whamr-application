01, Brand Essence

What the brand stands for

The name, the feeling, and the promise in three sentences.

💥

The Name

Wham is the sound of a perfect meme landing in the group chat. The full involuntary gut-reaction when someone sends exactly the right thing and everyone loses it. Whamr is named after what great content does to people.

🌍

The Mission

A global platform where people go to laugh, destress, and share joy. Content from every culture, language, and region, shared to WhatsApp, Telegram, X, and anywhere else in one tap. Free forever.

⚡

The Promise

Find the perfect meme in seconds. Share it anywhere in one tap. No accounts required, no ads, no friction. Just the wham, and then the send.

"Send the wham."

Tagline · used in all marketing, UI copy, and brand moments

02, Logo

The Whamr wordmark

The wordmark is lowercase, "whamr.", with the period as a deliberate design choice. The period signals impact, finality: it's been sent. Always use the full wordmark; never capitalize, never drop the period.

08, Usage Rules

What to do and not do

Simple rules that protect the brand at scale.

Always
  • ✓
    Use "whamr." lowercase, with the period
  • ✓
    Use the Sunset gradient for hero sections
  • ✓
    Use #FFD60A for virality/fire score badges
  • ✓
    Keep body text on warm cream, not cold white
  • ✓
    Use Space Grotesk for all headings and the logo
  • ✓
    Keep buttons orange-red on light backgrounds
  • ✓
    Use JetBrains Mono for all numbers and stats
Never
  • ✗
    Write "Whamr" (capital W) or "WHAMR"
  • ✗
    Drop the period: write "whamr" not "whamr."
  • ✗
    Use cold blue or purple as primary colors
  • ✗
    Place the orange-red logo on an orange-red background
  • ✗
    Use em dashes, in any copy or UI text
  • ✗
    Use the fire badge (#FFD60A) for non-virality purposes
  • ✗
    Mix font families outside their defined roles
