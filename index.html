About whamr

A meme tool, built for the way we actually share.

Most meme apps make you screenshot, save, then dig through your camera roll to send what you found. Whamr cuts the loop. You browse, you tap, the meme lands in WhatsApp ready to go.

Built mobile-first with a Naija-friendly library at its core. We’re starting with reactions, dance, awkward moments, and Nollywood favourites — the stuff people actually send their friends. More categories and creator tools come next.
